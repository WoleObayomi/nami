About
Nami is intended to be a flexible, customizable menu for use in your website. Out of the box, Nami provides the following:
  • A nav bar with display for showing the current location on the page
  • Support for submenus (nested as much as you want)
  • Sleek and attractive interface
In addition, Nami allows you to register events on your menu items so you can do almost anything!
